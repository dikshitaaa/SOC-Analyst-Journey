## SOC Analyst Roles

SOC team is divided into different levels based on responsibility
and experience.

---

###  L1 SOC Analyst (Entry Level)

 Responsibilities:
- Monitor security alerts from SIEM
- Identify false positives
- Perform basic investigation
- Escalate real threats to L2

 Focus:
Alert monitoring & triage

---

###  L2 SOC Analyst (Intermediate)

 Responsibilities:
- Deep log analysis
- Incident investigation
- Threat correlation
- Support incident response

 Focus:
Investigation & analysis

---

###  L3 SOC Analyst (Advanced)

 Responsibilities:
- Advanced threat hunting
- Malware analysis
- Detection rule creation
- Handle critical incidents

 Focus:
Advanced security operations
